Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you book your seat.

What's included in a conference ticket? +

Every ticket tier includes access to keynote sessions and the main talk tracks. Standard covers the core three days, while Pro and Team unlock workshops, mentorship, and recordings — check the pricing table above for the full breakdown.

Is there a virtual attendance option? +

Yes. Livestream passes go on sale six weeks before the event and include live Q&A with speakers plus access to session recordings for 30 days afterward.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket? +

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event and freely transferable to a colleague at any time — just email the organizers with the new attendee's details.

Do you offer student or group discounts? +

Students save 50% with a valid .edu email, and any group of 5 or more registering together receives a 20% discount automatically applied at checkout.

What should I bring on the day? +

Just your badge (emailed a week before the event) and a laptop for the hands-on workshops. Power strips, wifi, and coffee are all covered on-site.
